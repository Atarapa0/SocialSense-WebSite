Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to SocialSense. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Data Collection and Processing

SocialSense operates on a local-first principle. We do not store your Instagram login credentials or private data on our servers.

  • Instagram Data: The application analyzes the JSON data from your Instagram data export (ZIP file). This analysis happens entirely on your device. The parsed data is stored locally in your device's secure storage.
  • No Server Upload: Your Instagram data (followers, likes, comments history) is never uploaded to any external server. It remains on your phone.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for app stability and analytics:

  • Firebase Crashlytics: Used to detect crashes and improve app stability. Anonymous crash reports may be sent.
  • Google AdMob (if applicable): May be used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect data to serve personalized ads.

4. Data Retention

Since your parsed data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can delete your data at any time by uninstalling the application or using the "Clear Data" option within the app settings.
