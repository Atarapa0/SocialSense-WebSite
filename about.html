About Us

SocialSense is built with a simple mission: to give Instagram users complete ownership and insight into their own data, without compromising privacy.

We believe that your social media data belongs to you. That's why we built SocialSense as a local-first application. Unlike other analytics tools that require your login credentials and store your data on external servers, SocialSense processes everything right on your device.
