Data Deletion Instructions

SocialSense is designed with privacy in mind. We do not store your personal Instagram data on our servers.

How to Delete Your Data

Because your data is stored locally on your device, deleting it is simple and instant:

Option 1: Clear App Data

  • Open Settings in the SocialSense app.
  • Tap on "Clear All Data" or "Reset App".
  • Confirm the action. All parsed Instagram data will be permanently removed from your device storage.

Option 2: Uninstall the App

  • Long press the SocialSense app icon on your home screen.
  • Select Uninstall or Remove App.
  • This will remove the application and all associated data from your device immediately.
